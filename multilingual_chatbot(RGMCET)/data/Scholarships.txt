M.Tech, MBA & MCA Programmes
Stipend & Tuition Fee Reimbursement & Scholarships.

Students who are admitted on the basis of GATE rank are eligible for stipend from AICTE.

Students who are admitted based on PGECET rank are eligible for Tuition Fee Reimbursement from the Government of Andhra Pradesh if they satisfy the eligibility criteria.

Students who are admitted based on ICET rank are eligible for Tuition Fee Reimbursement from the Government of Andhra Pradesh if they satisfy the eligibility criteria.
