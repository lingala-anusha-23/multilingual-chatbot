Ph.D Programmes
Seven departments in the RGMCET namely CE, EEE, ME, ECE, Physics, Mathematics and MBA have been recognized as potential research centers by JNTUA, thereby enabling them to offer Doctoral Programmes.

For admission into Ph.D programmes, the process is prescribed by the affiliating University, JNTUA. The eligibility criteria is in accordance with the norms of the affiliating university.
